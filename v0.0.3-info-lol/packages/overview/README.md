# Overview – The Summoner's Chronicle Vision

## Purpose

To transform raw post-game statistics into a narrative-driven, visually compelling story of a single champion's performance, contextualized within the broader match dynamics. The graphic serves both as a learning tool (identifying strengths/weaknesses) and a celebratory artifact (highlighting exceptional plays).

## Target Audience

Players from Silver to Diamond who want actionable insights, plus casual viewers who appreciate dramatic data storytelling.

## Champion Selection

The graphic focuses on **one champion per player**, generated on-demand (player chooses which champion to analyze post-game). The system defaults to the player's played champion unless overridden.

## Tone

Analytical but accessible, dramatic but data-honest. Uses League lexicon without assuming deep statistical literacy.

## Boundaries

**Assumes from downstream packages:**
- Visual Design will create a compelling visual hierarchy that translates the narrative philosophy into concrete zones
- Statistics & Algorithms will provide calculations that are accurate, meaningful, and statistically sound
- Infrastructure will ensure data sources are reliable and privacy guarantees are honored

**Boundaries others must respect:**
- The purpose is narrative storytelling combined with learning, not raw statistical dumps
- All insights must be contextualized within the champion's role and the match state, not compared globally
- The product is player-centric (one champion, one match, one player) — not team-focused or matchup analysis
